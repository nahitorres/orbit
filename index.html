Good questions

The honest FAQ.

Is it really anonymous?

Yes. There's no account and no name. Your preferences live on your device, the map only ever shows aggregated crowds (minimum 10 people), and every signal expires. There's nothing to leak because we never hold it.

How is this different from Google Maps?

Maps tells you how busy a place is. ORBIT tells you what kind of crowd is there — and whether it's your scene — in real time.

Does it cost anything?

The map is free to try. A small optional pass — about €1 a night — keeps it ad-free and privacy-first, with no data ever sold.
